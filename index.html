Fun facts about Animals

How to make a camel.

  • Camels are domestic animals
  • Camels can go without water for 7 months
  • Camels live in hot locations

How to make a panda.

  • Pandas eat bamboo
  • Pandas are endangered animals
  • Pandas have white and black fur

How to make a teddy.

  • Teddy bears are fluffy
  • Teddy bears are brown,pink,white and many other colours
  • Many children own a teddy bear

How to make a cicada.

  • Cicadas can survive huge falls
  • Young cicadas are called nymphs
  • Cicadas are attracted to motor sounds

How to make a chameleon.

  • Chameleons can swivel their eyes
  • The panther chameleon changes its color to yellow or red when it's angry
  • Chameleons have a lightning-fast tongue

How to make a pigeon.

  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  • Pigeons mostly build nests around farms, warehouses, mills and grain storage
